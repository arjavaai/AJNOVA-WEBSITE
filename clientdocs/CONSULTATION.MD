# AjNova — Section 2: Free Consultation Form (UI Specification)

## Purpose
To make it effortless for students to request a free one-on-one consultation with an AjNova counsellor.
The page should feel calm, guided, and reassuring — the first real human touchpoint in the AjNova journey.

## UI Layout

### 1 Header
• Logo on the left.
• Navigation links: Home | About | Services | Eligibility Checker | Contact.
• Free Consultation button on the far right (consistent with other pages).

### 2 Hero Section
• Main heading: Book Your Free Consultation
• Sub-heading: Speak with our counsellors to plan your study pathway and clarify your next steps.
• Optional background photo or light illustration of students in discussion.

### 3 Consultation Form Block
**Title**: Consultation Request Form

**Form Fields**:
• Full Name
• Email Address
• Phone Number
• Preferred Course (dropdown: Engineering, Business, Health Sciences, Other)
• Intended Intake (dropdown: Winter 2025, Summer 2026)
• Message / Question (text area)
• Preferred Contact Time (dropdown: Morning, Afternoon, Evening)

**Action Button**: Submit Consultation Request

**Confirmation Message** (on submission):
"Thank you for your request! Our team will contact you within 24 hours with your consultation link."

### 4 What Happens Next
A short three-step visual strip:
1⃣ AjNova reviews your details.
2⃣ You receive a confirmation email with meeting link.
3⃣ You attend your free consultation to discuss your study goals.

### 5 FAQ (Optional)
Compact list of common questions such as:
• Is the consultation completely free?
• How long does it take?
• Can I reschedule if I am unavailable?

### 6 Footer
• Quick links (Home, About, Services, Contact).
• Social links (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube).
• Copyright line + AjNova tagline "Empowering Students for Global Success."

## User-Experience Guidelines
• Keep form compact (≤ 7 fields).
• Use warm, action-driven language.
• Show clear feedback after submission.
• Keep scroll depth minimal — everything visible within one screen on most devices.
• Maintain identical CTA phrasing across pages (Free Consultation).
