# AjNova — Section 1: Home / Contact Page (UI Specification + UX Enhancements)

## Purpose
The Home / Contact Page introduces AjNova and encourages visitors to engage — check eligibility, book a consultation, or send an inquiry.

## UI Layout

### 1 Header
• Logo on the left.
• Navigation: Home | About | Services | Eligibility Checker | Contact.
• Prominent Free Consultation button.

### 2 Hero Section
• Headline stating AjNova's mission.
• Supporting line summarizing the service value.
• Two CTAs: Check Eligibility and Book Consultation.
• Simple background image or illustration related to education.

### 3 Quick Eligibility Teaser
A small block inviting users:
"Find out if you qualify for German public universities – takes less than 60 seconds."

### 4 Success Snapshot
Slim banner with confidence metrics, for example:
"3 000 + Students Placed • 12 + Partner Universities • 97 % Visa Success Rate"

### 5 Highlights Section
Four short cards with icons and captions:
• German University Admissions Experts
• Complete Documentation Support
• Guidance for Public & Private Universities
• Personalized Counselling

### 6 How It Works
Three simple steps:
1 Check Eligibility 2 Schedule Consultation 3 Apply with Guidance

### 7 Contact Form Block
• Heading: "Let's Plan Your Study Journey Together."
• Fields: Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Message / Inquiry, Purpose Dropdown.
• CTA Button: Submit Inquiry
• Success Message: "Thank you for contacting AjNova! Our team will reach out soon."

### 8 Testimonials (Optional)
Carousel of 2–3 short student stories with names and photos / videos.

### 9 Free Resources
Small section with downloadable guides: Admission Checklist, SOP Samples, APS Guide.

### 10 Footer
Quick links (Home | About | Services | Contact) • Social icons • Partner logos (optional) • Copyright line and Policies.

### 11 Floating Help Button
Subtle "Need Guidance?" bubble leading to a mini FAQ or short form.

## UX Principles
• Use clear, friendly language.
• Limit forms to six fields or fewer.
• Make each section invite action or reassurance.
• Present social proof early.
• Keep hierarchy and spacing consistent.
• Evoke warmth and support — not sales.
