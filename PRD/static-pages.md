# Static Pages - UI/UX Specifications

## Document Overview
This document details the specifications for all public-facing static pages of the AJ NOVA platform, including the landing page, services page, and contact page.

---

## 1. Landing Page (Home Page)

### 1.1 Purpose
The landing page serves as the primary entry point for visitors. It should immediately communicate AJ NOVA's value proposition, build trust, and guide users toward key actions: checking eligibility, booking consultations, or learning more about services.

### 1.2 Page Structure

#### 1.2.1 Header Section
**Components:**
- **Logo:** AJ NOVA logo positioned on the left
- **Navigation Menu:** 
  - Home (active)
  - About
  - Services
  - Eligibility Checker
  - Contact
- **Call-to-Action Button:** "Free Consultation" (prominent, right-aligned)
- **Mobile Menu:** Hamburger menu for mobile devices

**Design Requirements:**
- Sticky header (remains visible on scroll)
- Clean, professional design
- Consistent across all pages

#### 1.2.2 Hero Section
**Content:**
- **Main Headline:** Compelling statement about studying in Germany
  - Example: "Your Gateway to German Universities"
- **Sub-headline:** Brief value proposition
  - Example: "Expert guidance, AI-powered tools, and personalized support for your study abroad journey"
- **Visual Elements:** 
  - Background image or illustration (education/study abroad theme)
  - Overlay for text readability
- **Call-to-Action Buttons:**
  - Primary: "Check Your Eligibility" (leads to eligibility checker)
  - Secondary: "Book Free Consultation" (leads to consultation page)

**Design Requirements:**
- Full-width section
- High visual impact
- Clear hierarchy
- Mobile-responsive

#### 1.2.3 Quick Eligibility Teaser
**Content:**
- **Text:** "Find out if you qualify for German public universities – takes less than 60 seconds"
- **Button:** "Check Eligibility Now"
- **Visual:** Small icon or illustration

**Purpose:** Quick conversion opportunity without leaving the page

#### 1.2.4 Success Metrics Snapshot
**Content:**
Display key statistics in a visually appealing banner:
- "3,000+ Students Placed"
- "12+ Partner Universities"
- "97% Visa Success Rate"
- "500+ Successful Applications"

**Design:** 
- Horizontal layout with icons
- Animated counters (optional)
- Trust-building element

#### 1.2.5 Key Highlights Section
**Content:**
Four feature cards with icons and brief descriptions:

1. **German University Admissions Experts**
   - Icon: Graduation cap or university building
   - Description: "Expert guidance for public and private universities"

2. **Complete Documentation Support**
   - Icon: Document/file icon
   - Description: "AI-powered SOP, LOR, and resume generation"

3. **Personalized Counselling**
   - Icon: Person/consultation icon
   - Description: "One-on-one support throughout your journey"

4. **Transparent Application Tracking**
   - Icon: Progress tracker icon
   - Description: "Real-time updates on your application status"

**Design:**
- Grid layout (2x2 on desktop, stacked on mobile)
- Consistent card styling
- Hover effects

#### 1.2.6 How It Works Section
**Content:**
Three-step process visualization:

**Step 1: Check Eligibility**
- Icon/Illustration
- Title: "Check Your Eligibility"
- Description: "Quick assessment to understand your options"

**Step 2: Schedule Consultation**
- Icon/Illustration
- Title: "Book Free Consultation"
- Description: "Speak with our expert counsellors"

**Step 3: Apply with Guidance**
- Icon/Illustration
- Title: "Complete Your Application"
- Description: "Get support through every step of the process"

**Design:**
- Horizontal flow with connecting lines/arrows
- Numbered steps
- Mobile: Vertical stack

#### 1.2.7 Services Preview Section
**Content:**
Brief overview of main services offered:
- Blocked Account
- Health Insurance
- Course Selection
- On-Arrival Services
- Loans
- Flight Bookings
- APS Verification

**Design:**
- Grid or carousel layout

#### 1.2.8 Testimonials Section (Optional)
**Content:**
- 2-3 student testimonials
- Student name, photo (optional), university enrolled

## 4. Design Guidelines for All Static Pages

### 4.1 Visual Design
- **Color Scheme:** Professional, student-friendly palette
- **Typography:** Clear, readable fonts with proper hierarchy
- **Spacing:** Generous white space for readability
- **Images:** High-quality, relevant images/illustrations
- **Icons:** Consistent icon set throughout

### 4.2 Responsive Design
- **Desktop:** Full-width layouts, multi-column grids
- **Tablet:** Adjusted layouts, maintained readability
- **Mobile:** Stacked layouts, touch-friendly buttons, optimized images

### 4.3 Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Alt text for all images
- Keyboard navigation support
- Sufficient color contrast
- Screen reader compatibility

### 4.4 Performance
- Optimized images (WebP format, lazy loading)
- Minimal JavaScript
- Fast page load times (< 2 seconds)
- Efficient CSS

### 4.5 Content Guidelines
- Clear, concise messaging
- Student-focused language
- Supportive and encouraging tone
- Action-oriented CTAs
- Multilingual support (English primary, German/Hindi secondary)

## 5. User Flow from Static Pages

### 5.1 Landing Page Flow
1. Visitor lands on homepage
2. Sees hero section and value proposition
3. Options:
   - Click "Check Eligibility" → Eligibility Checker
   - Click "Book Consultation" → Consultation Page
   - Scroll to learn more → Services/About sections
   - Fill contact form → Lead created in CRM

### 5.2 Services Page Flow
1. Visitor views services
2. Clicks on specific service
3. Options:
   - "Learn More" → Detailed service page (if exists)
   - "Get Started" → Consultation booking or contact form
   - "Book Consultation" → Consultation page

### 5.3 Contact Page Flow
1. Visitor wants to get in touch
2. Options:
   - Fill contact form → Lead created, email sent
   - Book consultation → Calendly integration
   - Call/Email directly → Direct contact
